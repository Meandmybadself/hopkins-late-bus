Privacy Policy

Late Bus Alert collects only two pieces of information:

  • Your email address
  • Your bus route number(s)

This data is stored solely to send you bus delay notifications. We will never share, sell, or distribute your information to anyone.

You can delete all of your data at any time by unsubscribing. This immediately and permanently removes your email and route information from our system.
